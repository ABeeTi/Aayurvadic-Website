About Thr Company

Established in 2007 with the aim to provide accessible ayurvedic(herbal) medicines and supplements to the Nepalese market, Gorakhnath Harbaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. has grown to become a leading manufacturer and supplier in a relatively short span of time. With over 250 registered products that fullfull the GMP regulations, we currently actively produce 50 and are constantly adding new products to this growing list. Our product segments include capsules, powder, granules, oils, bhasma, bati, paste and so on. All of our products are classical and proprietary and very well-accepted in the market.
Our mai manufacturing plant is located in Kavre district of Nepal and we are registered with the Department of Drug Administration(DDA) within the Health Ministry of the Government of Nepal. All our products are supplied nationally from Kavre.
